Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

1. Who we are

Adsoleo ("we", "us") is a creator-management agency. Our registered office is Adsoleo SAS, 10 Rue de la Paix, 75002 Paris, France. You can reach us at privacy@adsoleo.com.

2. What this policy covers

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal data when you use the Adsoleo mobile app and any associated services (the "Services"). It does not cover third-party websites we link to.

3. The data we collect

Category Examples Why we collect it
Account Email, password hash, role (Admin / Creator) To authenticate you and run the app.
Profile (Creators) Photo, public name, bio, country, city, languages, categories, social usernames + follower counts, engagement rate, portfolio media So clients can evaluate you for campaigns.
Applications Pitch text, content idea, availability, status To process your application to a campaign.
Device FCM push token, platform (iOS / Android) To send you push notifications.
Diagnostic Crash reports, performance traces (via Sentry) To diagnose and fix bugs.
Communication Phone number (optional, for SMS) To send selection / approval SMS.

We do not collect: precise location, contacts, or advertising IDs.

4. Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Contract: to provide the service you signed up for (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Legitimate interest: to keep the platform secure and fix bugs (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Consent: for optional push notifications and SMS (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR — you can withdraw via OS settings and the in-app toggle).

5. How we share data

  • Clients of Adsoleo see your profile only via a secure private link generated by the admin. They do not see your email, phone, or password. The link expires after 7 or 14 days and the admin can deactivate it sooner.
  • Service providers: SendGrid (email), Twilio (SMS), Firebase (push), Render (hosting), Sentry (error reporting). Each is bound by a Data Processing Agreement.
  • No advertising networks. No data brokers. No sale of personal data.

6. Where we store data

EU-region servers via Render. Backups encrypted at rest. Transfers to non-EU sub-processors (e.g., Sentry US) are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses.

7. How long we keep it

Data Retention
Active account While the account is active.
Closed account 30 days, then irreversibly deleted (except where law requires retention).
Application + decision records 2 years (for billing/dispute reasons).
Diagnostic data 90 days.
Backups 30 days.

8. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct your data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data (data portability)
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority, e.g., the CNIL (Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés) in France.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@adsoleo.com. We respond within 30 days.

9. Children

Adsoleo is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Security

  • All traffic over TLS.
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt (cost ≥ 12).
  • JWTs signed and short-lived; refresh tokens rotated on use.
  • Device tokens stored in OS secure storage (Keychain / Keystore).
  • Access to production data is limited to 2 authorized engineers, and is audited.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes are announced in-app at least 14 days before they take effect.
